# 🍔 AL-Hani Fast Food | Digital Transformation

**A Modern Web Platform for Karachi's Favorite Flavors**
*Developed FastFood ordering website for client*

---

## 🌟 The Project

I have developed this comprehensive web platform for **AL-Hani Fast Food** to bring their legendary taste to the digital world. The goal was to create a high-performance, visually stunning interface that captures the essence of their brand while providing a seamless ordering experience for the Karachi market.

---

## 🍽️ What You Can Do in This Web App

This platform is designed to be highly functional and user-centric. Here’s what customers and staff can experience:

*   **Explore the Menu**: Effortlessly browse through a wide variety of categories including Burgers, Sandwiches, the famous Shapatar Rolls, Pizzas, and more.
*   **Curate Your Order**: Add multiple items to your real-time shopping cart. The system automatically calculates your subtotal as you go.
*   **Instant WhatsApp Ordering**: Instead of complex checkout forms, users can place their entire order directly to the business WhatsApp. The system generates a clean, professional order summary automatically.
*   **Get in Touch Directly**: Customers can send inquiries or feedback that open instantly as formatted WhatsApp messages, ensuring no lead is ever lost.
*   **Locate the Store**: An interactive Google Maps integration helps customers find the Coast Guard Chowrangi, Korangi location with a single click.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

*   **⚡ Blazing Fast Performance**: Built with **Vite** and **React 18** for near-instant page loads.
*   **🛒 Persistent Cart System**: Uses `localStorage` so that if a user closes the tab, their selected food stays in the cart for their next visit.
*   **✨ Premium Micro-Animations**: Powered by **Framer Motion**, featuring floating food cards, smooth scroll effects, and hover-triggered glassmorphism.
*   **📱 Fully Responsive Design**: Perfectly optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop—crucial for on-the-go food ordering.
*   **✅ Reliable Validation**: Secure form handling using **React Hook Form** and **Zod** to ensure all customer data (Name, Phone, Address) is accurate before an order is placed.
*   **🎨 Custom Brand Aesthetics**: A deep-tinted dark mode theme with vibrant primary accents, tailored to make food imagery pop and feel premium.
*   **📦 Lightweight & Clean Code**: I've conducted a full audit to remove redundant components and legacy files, keeping the project extremely maintainable.
